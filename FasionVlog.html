FASHION - - -

The French word mode, meaning "fashion", dates as far back as 1482,

while the English word denoting something "in style" dates only to the 16th century.

Fashion is a term used interchangeably to describe the creation of clothing,

footwear, accessories, cosmetics, and jewellery of different cultural aesthetic

and their mix and match into outfits that depict distinctive ways of dressing as

signifiers of social status, self-expression, and group belonging. As a

multifaceted term, fashion describes an industry, styles, aesthetics, and trends.
